Air Freight Forwarding

Description: Air Freight Forwarding involves the coordination and shipment of goods via aircraft. This logistics service is essential for businesses needing quick, reliable delivery of their products across long distances. Air freight forwarding includes the management of all necessary documentation, customs clearance, and adherence to international regulations, ensuring a seamless and efficient shipping process.

Ocean Freight Forwarding

Description: Ocean Freight Forwarding is the process of managing and coordinating the transportation of goods by sea. This service is crucial for companies that require cost-effective and efficient shipment of large volumes of cargo over long distances. Ocean freight forwarding encompasses the handling of all necessary documentation, customs clearance, and compliance with international maritime regulations, ensuring a smooth and reliable delivery process.

Road Freight Forwarding

Description: Road Freight Forwarding involves the organization and transportation of goods via trucks and other road vehicles. This logistics service is vital for businesses needing flexible, door-to-door delivery solutions within and across regions. Road freight forwarding includes the management of all required documentation, route planning, and regulatory compliance, ensuring efficient and timely delivery of cargo.

Expedited Services

Description: Expedited Services provide fast and prioritized transportation solutions for urgent shipments. This service is essential for businesses needing time-sensitive delivery of goods, ensuring they reach their destination as quickly as possible. Expedited services include specialized handling, priority routing, and often real-time tracking, guaranteeing a swift and reliable delivery process.

Our Story

Glory Cargo Asia Sdn Bhd is a well-diversified total logistics provider of integrated freight, forwarding, customs clearance, high-tech warehousing, interstate and local bonded trucking and container haulage services. Established since 2018, Glory Cargo Asia is a 100% wholly owned Malaysian company. The company’s activities are conducted and managed through its head office in Sepang, Selangor and operation offices at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport, Penang Airport, Kota Kinabalu Airport and Johor Bahru.
